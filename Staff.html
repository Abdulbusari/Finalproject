Khadijat Busari

Daycare Owner

She is a Canadian Citizen born in Ibadan, the largest city in West Africa, Nigeria, and a mother of four lovely children, two girls and boys, with a wonderful husband. She obtained a Nigerian Certificate in Education in 1999. she then proceeded to university and received a Bachelor of Education in Guidance and Counselling 2003. She is an internationally trained teacher with over 15 years experience, before relocating with my family to Winnipeg, Manitoba in July 2011. Here in Winnipeg, she pursued a Diploma in Early Childhood Education at Red River College, Winnipeg, Manitoba and graduated in year 2014. After graduation she worked as a Childcare Program Manager at the Immigrant and Refugee Community Organization of Manitoba. In 2013 she joined YMCA-YWCA as Early Childhood Educator II until she decided to start my own family daycare. she also has special training with Family Dynamics in affiliation with Red River College as Family Childcare Provider. She is a member of the Manitoba Childcare Association with years of experiences as a childcare provider. Khadijat is currently pursuing a degree in Bachelor of Arts Developmental Studies and Child Development/Care at University of Winnipeg.
