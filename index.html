➣ A licensed family Child Care that offers a play-based learning of experiences for young children in a safe and nuturing enviroment.
➣ Passionate about working in partnership with families in creating a conducive learning enviroment.
➣ Proficient in helping children attain their physical, cognitive, social and emotional development by getting them ready for the present and the future.

What we offer!!

At Adorable Child Care it is about creating an optimal environment where children can develop intellectually, socially, physically and emotionally; uniquely prepared environment incorporating holistic learning tailored to individual child developmental needs and interest based on their sensitive periods.

Preschool

18 months - 4 years

Our preschool program is designed to allow children to study alongside them. We utilise the children's interests as indications for programming directions and expand on their curiosity. This curriculum is fully unstructured, allowing children to learn and grow at their own speed without fear of being judged.

Nursery School

4 years

Our redesigned nursery education program aids in the development of children's school ready skills. Pre-literacy skills, building self-reliance and independence, and socialising via play are all topics we focus on. To better prepare them for a successful introduction into the school system, we urge youngsters to begin developing problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

>

School Age

5 years - 12 years

Our school-aged curriculum is meant to give kids an open-ended area to explore the new and exciting ideas and concepts they're learning about every day. We use group projects and clubs to focus on greater long-term learning objectives here. We plan a variety of activities during the summer and during school breaks, ranging from field trips to art and craft classes.
